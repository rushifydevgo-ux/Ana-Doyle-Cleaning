About Kevin

Four decades in, still on every job site.

Kevin's career started in the mid-1980s painting tract homes across Silicon Valley. From there he joined Local Union 506, building journeyman-level skills on commercial and industrial projects, before eventually branching out to run his own jobs. Kevin Johnson Painting has been serving San Jose as a licensed contracting business since 2002.

Every client gets a paint consultation up front — so they know exactly what materials and finish they're paying for — and a full walkthrough when the job is done, not just an invoice.

"Painting isn't how you get rich — it's a great way to make a living. I learned from my grandmother growing up, and I come from a whole line of painters. I'm still in love with what I do."

— Kevin Johnson, Owner
